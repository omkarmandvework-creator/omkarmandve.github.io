ERP CRP & Fit-Gap Playbook

Module-wise CRP execution, issue triage, and fit-gap documentation for clean ERP deployments.

Oracle ERP CRP / Fit-Gap Panaya Tax • CE • Expenses

Project Overview

This playbook standardizes how I plan and run Conference Room Pilot (CRP) sessions across core Oracle ERP modules, capture gaps and defects, and convert findings into configuration updates and UAT-ready outcomes.

Scope

End-to-end CRP preparation, workshop facilitation, gap capture, defect triage, and remediation tracking across Finance & Compliance modules.

Approach

Structured agendas + scripts + data sets, scenario walk-throughs with business SMEs, fit-gap classification, and prioritized closure aligned to release and cutover readiness.

Outcome

Reduced rework, earlier issue discovery, aligned stakeholders, and higher confidence in go-live readiness through repeatable module-level governance.

Module Tabs

Each tab below summarizes CRP focus areas, typical gaps, and the artifacts produced for that module.

General Ledger (GL)

Validate Chart of Accounts structure, ledgers, calendars, journals, allocations, and financial reporting flow.

CRP Focus

  • Ledger setup, accounting calendar, currencies & rates
  • Journal entry lifecycle: create → approve → post → reverse
  • Intercompany & balancing (where applicable)
  • Period close steps, reconciliations, and audit evidence

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • COA segment governance & reporting granularity
  • Approval rules, journal sources/categories mismatches
  • Close dependencies across subledgers

Artifacts

  • GL CRP script (journals + close)
  • COA validation checklist
  • GL Fit-Gap log template

KPIs

  • Journal cycle time
  • Close readiness score
  • Open gaps aging

Tax

Validate tax determination rules, regimes/rates, reporting requirements, and tax accounting impact across AP/AR.

CRP Focus

  • Tax regimes, statuses, rates, jurisdiction logic
  • Place of supply / ship-to bill-to logic (as applicable)
  • Input vs output tax accounting and recoverability
  • Tax invoices, rounding rules, exemptions, reverse charge

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Business rule exceptions not covered in standard setup
  • Tax reporting gaps (registers, reconciliation needs)
  • Edge cases: credit memos, advances, mixed supplies

Artifacts

  • Tax configuration matrix
  • Tax scenarios & expected results
  • Tax Fit-Gap log + evidence pack

Tools

  • Oracle Tax
  • Subledger Accounting (SLA)
  • Panaya triage

Accounts Payable (AP)

Validate supplier lifecycle, invoices, approvals, payments, withholding, and accounting outcomes.

CRP Focus

  • Supplier onboarding controls, payment methods, bank details
  • Invoice capture: PO vs non-PO, matching, holds
  • Approval workflows, payment batches, bank file outputs
  • Tax/withholding handling and accounting validation

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Approval hierarchies vs business delegations
  • Invoice exceptions / holds policies not aligned
  • Payment file formats and bank connectivity constraints

Artifacts

  • AP CRP script (invoice→payment)
  • Payment run checklist
  • AP Fit-Gap + defect register

Controls

  • Maker-checker approvals
  • Duplicate invoice prevention
  • Audit trail readiness

Accounts Receivable (AR)

Validate billing, receipts, credit management, revenue accounting touchpoints, and customer statements.

CRP Focus

  • Customer setup, payment terms, tax profiles
  • Invoice creation, credit memos, adjustments
  • Receipts: lockbox/manual, application, reversals
  • Customer aging, statements, and dispute handling

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Receipt matching rules and reference quality
  • Write-off/adjustment approvals
  • Reporting needs for collections and reconciliation

Artifacts

  • AR CRP script (invoice→receipt)
  • Receipts matching scenarios
  • AR Fit-Gap log template

KPIs

  • DSO / collections
  • Unapplied receipts
  • Aging accuracy

Cash Management (CE)

Validate bank accounts, statement imports, reconciliation rules, and cash positioning for finance control.

CRP Focus

  • Bank accounts, formats, statement import/translation
  • Auto/Manual reconciliation rules and exceptions
  • Cash in transit visibility (AR/AP clearing timing)
  • Bank charges, interest, and accounting impact

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Bank statement format constraints and data quality
  • Matching rules tuning for high-volume transactions
  • Reconciliation reporting expectations

Artifacts

  • CE reconciliation playbook
  • Auto-match rule catalogue
  • CE Fit-Gap + defect tracker

Governance

  • Daily recon cadence
  • Exception SLA
  • Audit evidence pack

Expenses (Exp)

Validate travel & expense policy, approvals, corporate card integration, reimbursements, and accounting.

CRP Focus

  • Spend authorization, cash advances, policy enforcement
  • Expense report creation, approvals, audit rules
  • Reimbursements: payment method, timing, exceptions
  • Corporate card feeds and reconciliation

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Policy edge cases (per diem, exceptions, caps)
  • Approval delegation and escalations
  • Tax handling on expenses and compliance evidence

Artifacts

  • Expenses process flows + scripts
  • Policy-to-system mapping sheet
  • Exp Fit-Gap + UAT scenarios

KPIs

  • Approval cycle time
  • Audit exception rate
  • Reimbursement SLA

Fixed Assets (FA)

Validate asset lifecycle, capitalization, depreciation rules, retirements, and reporting across books.

CRP Focus

  • Asset categories, books (corporate/IFRS/tax), conventions
  • Additions: manual, mass additions from AP, CIP flow
  • Depreciation runs, revaluations, impairments, transfers
  • Retirements/disposals and accounting validation

Typical Fit-Gaps

  • Multi-book reporting alignment and localized rules
  • CIP-to-asset controls and evidence requirements
  • Depreciation policy exceptions and cutover data needs

Artifacts

  • FA CRP script (lifecycle)
  • Multi-book rule matrix
  • FA Fit-Gap + cutover checklist

Controls

  • Capitalization approvals
  • Book close governance
  • Audit-ready reporting

Responsibilities

  • CRP planning & facilitation (Finance modules)
  • Fit-gap documentation & prioritization
  • Issue triage and defect lifecycle in Panaya
  • Stakeholder alignment & closure governance

Artifacts

  • Master Playbook Document
  • CRP Session Template
  • Fit-Gap Log (Excel)
  • UAT Scenario Pack
