About Me

Finance professional, engineer and continuous learner.

I am a finance consultant and CFA Level I candidate with a background in mechanical engineering. My passion lies in building scalable valuation models, automating finance workflows and translating complex data into actionable insights.

Over the years I have delivered global Oracle ERP Finance implementations at Infosys, executed property valuations for governmental clients and founded a startup during the COVID‑19 pandemic. These experiences taught me the value of rigour, adaptability and long‑term thinking.

My long‑term goal is to work at the intersection of fintech, venture capital and hedge funds. I thrive on combining analytical discipline with a sense of curiosity, and I draw inspiration from archetypes like Ravana – not in mythological reverence but as a symbol of intellect, conviction and the courage to challenge convention.

Outside work, I enjoy writing about economics, markets and policy; exploring new cultures; and refining my understanding of emerging technologies. I’m always eager to connect with like‑minded professionals and explore opportunities to make finance smarter, more efficient and impactful.
